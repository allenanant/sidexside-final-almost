How much does SideXSide cost?
+

SideXSide offers a 14-day free trial so you can experience all features risk-free. After that, it's just $12/month to stay connected with walking companions on campus.

How does verification work?
+

We verify students through selfie matching, student ID checks, and phone number confirmation. This ensures everyone on the platform is who they say they are.

What if I'm not feeling the vibe?
+

No problem. You can end the walk anytime. Tap the Quick Help button to discreetly notify your trusted contacts. Our support team is always available.
